Dr. Benazir Nazar , CEO Akbar Holidays

A maverick in leisure travel, Benazir Nazar has had, what many would call, an interesting journey. Armed with a B.Sc. in Business Administration and an L.L.B in Corporate Law from Cardiff University one of the most prestigious institutions in the United Kingdom, Earned the title of Doctor of Letters from Kings University for her outstanding contribution towards the Travel and Tourism Industry.
With dreams of gavels and courtrooms, she relocated to Mumbai and had a stint at one of India’s leading law firms, DKS Legal, before finding herself at the Akbar Group. The largest travel conglomerate in the country, the Akbar Group had only just launched its online avatar and Benzy found herself driving the group’s travel portal, akbartravels.com. It didn’t take her long to realize she had found her calling. Travel was where she belonged. Five years at akbartravels.com and she was ready for the next step in her career. 2012 saw the birth of Akbar Holidays Pvt Ltd, an organization that would soon become one of the biggest names in leisure travel. She had finally arrived. As Chief Executive Officer, she has been instrumental in making Akbar Holidays the Indian traveller’s first choice for bespoke holiday packages customized for every budget and palate. Known as much for her tenacity as her brilliance, her vision and resolve made the company what it is today.
A winner of the Young Women’s Entrepreneur Award and Abacus Entrepreneur Award, Benzy and her entrepreneurial spirit have not gone unnoticed. She was won Akbar Holidays the unique distinction of being “Top Selling Agents for Hong Kong 3 years in a row and Top Selling Agent for Thailand.
